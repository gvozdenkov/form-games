Who we need

We need an analyst who will actively collaborate with the B2B team, knows sales processes like the back of their hand, supports B2B partners, and who will be helping the business to search for answers in data and then make decisions based on the data. If you are interested in deeply immersing yourself in the work of a modern IT company, are keen on actively developing as a professional, and enjoy making discoveries, then this vacancy is just the ticket for you!

Job responsibilities

  • Study the business processes of the Sales and Account Managers, assign metrics to these business processes, and find ways to optimize them in the data;
  • Conduct ad hoc data research on the behavior of B2B users and partners; describe the results of the research in an understandable language;
  • Compile technical tasks for tools in Tableau and help the BI developer to approve data layouts and prepare data;
  • Help managers prepare the data that decisions will be based on (SQL queries, Excel);
  • Manage separate B2B projects: think up optimal solutions for tasks together with other teams, keep project records, and set tasks for external teams in order to change or create tools for receiving data (CRM, Partners).

Examples of projects that you will be working on:

  1. An analysis of the current structure of the portfolio of partners; segmentation of B2B partners and prioritizing the order in which they are processed by the Account Managers;
  2. Optimizing the bonus program for Sales and Account Managers;
  3. An analysis of the current structure of the portfolio of partners; segmentation of B2B partners and prioritizing the order in which they are processed by the Account Managers.

Key qualifications

At least 1 year of work experience in analytics or experience with data analysis received during other positions:

  • The desire to grow, learn something new, and take on responsibility;
  • Curiosity and persistence;
  • An idea of how business works and which metrics are generally used to assess it;
  • Knowledge of SQL;
  • Experience working with BI (business intelligence) tools (we work with Tableau);
  • Knowledge of the fundamentals of statistics (median, cumulative distribution functions, p-value, confidence interval);
  • Python (pandas, numpy, seaborn, matplotlib, scikit-learn, statsmodels);
  • Good skills in MS Excel;
  • English - Upper Intermediate.

It’s a bonus if you also have:

  • Experience writing technical specifications;
  • An idea of how databases are structured (we work with Vertica).

We offer you

  • Remote work;
  • A flexible timetable — we don’t require you to be online at 09:00 sharp. You can start work at a time that suits you;
  • Interesting and ambitious tasks that will take you to the next professional level;
  • Learning: seminars, trainings and conferences. If you want to participate in a conference,we will help to organize it;
  • Private health insurance;
  • Team-building activities: movie nights, quizzes, thematic parties, annual trips to the countryside, football and volleyball matches;
  • Corporate discounts on hotels and other services;
  • A young and active team of super specialists.

Product

Our solutions empower hoteliers to effortlessly showcase their accommodations, boosting visibility and attracting a broader audience.
